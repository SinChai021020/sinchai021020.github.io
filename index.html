About YPC Foodie

YPC Foodie Guide is a student-led digital platform specifically designed for the YPC International College community. Our story began with a common daily struggle: wandering through Pudu unsure where to find a meal that was both easy on the wallet and high in quality.

Our team established three core pillars for our platform: Value, Proximity, and Authenticity. Every recommendation featured has been personally visited and price-verified. We prioritize traditional stalls and modern cafes within a 10-minute walk.

Student Budget Tips

  • 1. The "Off-Peak" Strategy

    Arrive before 11:30 AM or after 1:45 PM to avoid queues and get fresher batches of economy rice.

  • 2. Maximize the Free Perks

    Prioritize traditional Pudu shops that offer unlimited free daily soup and Chinese tea for dine-in.

  • 3. The "Kuah" (Gravy) Trick

    At mixed rice stalls, always ask for free curry or dahl gravy on your rice for extra flavor without extra cost.
